Embodied Making Analyst Analyst Course - starting at 1st of September 2015

What is the course about?

You will get to know Embodied Making in theory and practice. You will learn to apply all Embodied Making’s practices to a complex challenge. The discussion of real cases will give you a feel for the applicability of Embodied Making and you will apply Embodied Making to your own work. As a result of the course you will have a thorough understanding of Embodied Making and you will be able to apply it to real situations. After this course, your creations will have more impact.

Why me?

Because now is your time. We are all creators, and our creations have brought us far. But we all know that often our creations have unintended consequences. It is urgent that we learn to create things that are beautiful, useful, and sustainable. It is urgent that we learn to embrace complexity. And it is urgent that the things we create address the real needs of our planet. With Embodied Making, all this can be fun, fulfilling, and fast.

Course objectives

This is a foundation course introducing Embodied Making to practicing professionals with at least 3 years of work xperience. It covers all of the basics of embodied making and prepares practitioners for applying Embodied Making in any environment. All material in the course is in English. The course can also be offered in Dutch or German if required.

Course duration

This is a 5 day course conducted for 6 hours a day from 9 am to 17 pm with breaks for lunch and coffee (included in the course pricing).
