Why me?

Because now is your time. We are all creators, and our creations have brought us far. But we all know that often our creations have unintended consequences. It is urgent that we learn to create things that are beautiful, useful, and sustainable. It is urgent that we learn to embrace complexity. And it is urgent that the things we create address the real needs of our planet. With Embodied Making, all this can be fun, fulfilling, and fast.
